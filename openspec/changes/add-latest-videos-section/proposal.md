## Why

Visitors need a quick way to recognize the channel's recent content from the landing page before committing to the full YouTube channel. Showing the latest published videos makes the page feel current and gives visitors a direct path into recent material.

## What Changes

- Add a visible latest videos section to the landing page.
- Display the four most recent published videos as visually distinct video items.
- Make each video item clickable so visitors can open the corresponding YouTube video.
- Label the section clearly as recent channel content.
- Preserve responsive behavior so the section follows the provided desktop and mobile design direction.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `latest-videos`: Covers discovery of recent channel videos, visible video representations, and click-through behavior from the landing page.

### Modified Capabilities
- None.

## Impact

- Landing page presentation and responsive layout.
- Public YouTube RSS feed consumption for recent video metadata.
- Browser navigation to YouTube video URLs.
- Unit and/or browser-level coverage for rendering, ordering, and click-through behavior.
